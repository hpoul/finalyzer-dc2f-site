Import your personal portfolio from Yahoo! Finance

July 29, 2018

You might have used various other tools to track your personal investments before finding Anlage.App. So to jump start your experience we provide various imports. Now you can also import your existing Yahoo! Finance portfolio!

Export your stock purchases

Log into your Yahoo! Finance account and select the stock portfolio you want to transfer from My Portfolio.

Then above the list of your stocks you will see a couple of options. Simply select Export and download the CSV file.

With the downloaded CSV file simply log into your Anlage.App account and hit the Import Portfolio (CSV File) button!

If you stumble upon any problems, don’t hesitate to contact us.
